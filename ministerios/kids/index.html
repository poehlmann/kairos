CGK Four Foundational Simple Truths

We believe that the Bible is God’s Word and as we explore and study it, we will continually be pointed towards these four foundational simple truths that are found throughout the entirety of God’s word.

God is Good

Select tab

God is good in His nature as demonstrated by His grace, mercy, love, and faithfulness throughout the whole of the Bible.

God is in Charge

Select tab

God is in charge over all of His creation, reigning and ruling for our good and His glory.

God is the Savior

Select tab

God is the one and only Savior of the world that all people are invited to place faith in for the forgiveness of their sins and the promise of eternal life.

I Can Know God

Select tab

God is inviting each of us to have a personal relationship with Him as adopted children through faith in Jesus Christ.

CGK Sunday Morning Rooms

Our desire is for kids to learn Simple Truths and be inspired to grow a Simple Faith in the context of consistent relationships. We offer rooms based on age and grade so kids can learn age appropriate truths and build relationships with their peers. Individual rooms have a maximum capacity to maintain safe volunteer to student ratios and promote great growing environments.

Child Protection Policy

First Time Visitors

We look forward to meeting you in the kids wing located right in the doors from the north parking lot! Our kids coach will then help you get your kids registered and checked into their classroom and provide you with their name tags and security codes needed for pick up. We will also collect your contact information in case we need to reach you during the service. All of our classrooms are led by faithful volunteers who have been annually trained and background checked. Your kids will have the opportunity to learn one of Four Foundational Simple Truth’s from the Bible, worship through song and dance, and build relationships with their peers and volunteers.

CGK Welcome Booklet

Volunteers

We believe that parents have the wonderful responsibility of making disciples of their children! However they are not alone in this and the church is tasked to come alongside them through Gospel Inspired Friendships! Cottage Grove Kids relies on a faithful and loving team of volunteers who desire to see the kids in and around our church Know, Love, and Follow Jesus!

All of our volunteers undergo annual training, pass an annual background check, and agree to uphold our Child Protection Policy before working with children.
