Meet & Connect Every Week

Each week smaller groups of people meet in homes in the surrounding communities of Des Moines. These Connection Groups seek to bring together people in various stages of life, from various demographics, with different backgrounds and stories in order to encourage, support and teach one another the truth about who God is and what that means for us personally.

Our hope is that each Connection Group is a space for the people in it to:

  1. Know God and know one another.
  2. Care for, share with, and invite neighbors.
  3. Send out leaders to start more life giving communities.
