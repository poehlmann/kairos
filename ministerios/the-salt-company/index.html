The Salt Company

Thursdays at 8:00p

Salt Company is the college ministry of Cottage Grove Church and exists to proclaim and display new life in Christ. Our desire is for college students in Des Moines to embrace the good news of Jesus Christ, grow in their love of God and people, and to change the world as the “salt of the earth”.

“You are the Salt of the Earth” - Matthew 5:13

What We Do

Worship Gatherings

We value coming together to worship Jesus through song and hearing about Him through messages from the Bible. We meet weekly at 8:00p at Cottage Grove Church! Come and join us.

Small Groups

We also value sharing our lives with each other, reading from the Bible, strengthening friendships and having fun. Jump into a student led small group today!

Prayer Requests

We as the elders of Cottage Grove Church are commissioned to do two primary things, according to the scriptures. That is to teach the Word of God and to pray for the saints (Acts 6:2-4 ESV). What we would like to do is provide you a space where you can reach out to the elders and request prayer for specific needs or desires.

We commit to raising up these requests before the Lord on your behalf and will follow-up with you when the opportunity presents itself.
